Peanut Soup

An interesting and exotic soup that's also very rich.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons margarine
  • 3 tablespoons minced onion
  • clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • Â½ cup ground peanuts

Directions

  1. In a heavy soup pot melt the butter. Stir until very smooth.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, flour and peanut butter.
  3. Beat in the stock and season well with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer over low heat until thick, about 20 minutes.
  5. Stir in the cream and heat through.
  6. Serve warm with ground peanuts.
